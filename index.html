About Me

Hi! My name is Andrei and I am a research associate at MIT, which is pretty much just a fancy way of saying post-doc. My work consists mostly on research related to decentralization and data-ownship on the Web. Until very recently, I lead a really cool project called Solid, under the careful supervision of Prof. Tim Berners-Lee. The project aims to radically change the way Web applications work today, resulting in true data ownership as well as improved privacy.

I am also a W3C Team member, where I participate in Web standards work on topics ranging from identity, authentication and also Semantic Web technologies such as LDP and LDPatch. You will find more details in the Activity section below.
